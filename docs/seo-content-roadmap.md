## Operational Notes

- Keep payment-date pages programmatic where possible and use guide pages only when the search intent is explanatory.
- For navigational keywords that clearly want the official portal, publish independent helper pages that explain the official route and link out safely instead of trying to capture the action itself.
- Reuse the existing tone:
  - short paragraphs
  - plain language
  - no official impersonation
  - soft CTA only
- Future publishing order:
  1. Batch 9 for fast traffic expansion
  2. Batch 6 for high-volume navigational-intent coverage
  3. Batch 8 for high-problem-resolution intent
  4. Batch 7 for appeals
  5. Batch 10 and Batch 11 for evergreen depth
