# 📄 Cover Letter Template Pack (South Africa Optimised)

## 🧠 Overview
A ready-to-use cover letter template pack designed to help job seekers quickly create professional, compelling cover letters for different job applications.

This product removes the stress of “what do I say?” by providing **pre-written, editable letters** tailored to common job scenarios in South Africa.

---

## 🎯 Target Audience
- First-time job seekers
- Students applying for internships
- Individuals applying for multiple jobs daily
- Users from GrantCare looking for employment opportunities

---

## 💰 Monetization Strategy
- Single pack: R39 – R99
- Bundle with CV templates: R149+
- Upsell:
  - Custom cover letter service (R100 – R250)
  - CV + Cover Letter combo (R199+)

---

## 📦 What’s Included (Product Contents)

### 1. Cover Letter Templates (Editable)
- 5–8 professionally written templates
- Formats:
  - Microsoft Word (.docx)
  - PDF preview versions

---

### 2. Job Scenario Templates
Each template must target a specific situation:

- No experience (first job)
- Retail / general worker
- Admin / office jobs
- Call centre / customer service
- Internship applications
- Career change

---

### 3. Fill-in-the-Blank Sections
Make it extremely easy:

- [Your Name]
- [Company Name]
- [Position Applied For]
- [Your Skills]

👉 Users should only replace a few fields and send

---

### 4. Email Version Templates
Include short versions for email applications:

- “Application for [Job Title]”
- Professional body text ready to paste

---

### 5. Step-by-Step Guide (PDF)

Guide should include:
- How to customise your letter
- What employers look for
- Mistakes to avoid
- When to use each template
