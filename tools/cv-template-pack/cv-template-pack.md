# 📄 CV Template Pack (South Africa Optimised)

## 🧠 Overview
A professionally designed, easy-to-use CV (Resume) template pack tailored specifically for South African job seekers. This product is designed to help users quickly create a high-quality CV that increases their chances of getting shortlisted for interviews.

This is a **high-conversion digital product** targeted at students, unemployed individuals, and professionals seeking better job opportunities.

---

## 🎯 Target Audience
- Students applying for internships or first jobs
- Unemployed individuals actively job hunting
- Professionals looking to upgrade their CVs
- Users from GrantCare platform seeking employment

---

## 💰 Monetization Strategy
- Single pack: R49 – R99
- Bundle with cover letters: R149+
- Upsell:
  - CV review service (R150 – R300)
  - Done-for-you CV (R300+)

---

## 📦 What’s Included (Product Contents)

### 1. CV Templates (Editable)
- 3–5 modern CV designs
- Formats:
  - Microsoft Word (.docx)
  - PDF (example preview)
- Styles:
  - Simple/clean (ATS-friendly)
  - Modern/professional
  - Entry-level focused

### 2. Pre-Written Content Sections

Include:

- Education descriptions

### 3. Industry-Specific Examples

- Admin jobs

### 4. Step-by-Step Guide (PDF)
Guide should include:

- Common mistakes to avoid
