# 📄 Fix Declined Application Guide + Templates (South Africa Optimised)

## 🧠 Overview
A complete, step-by-step system designed to help users understand why their application was declined and exactly how to fix it.

This product combines **education + action + templates**, giving users everything they need to correct their application and reapply successfully.

It is positioned as a **solution product**, not just templates.

---

## 🎯 Target Audience
- Users whose SASSA or grant applications were declined
- Individuals confused about rejection reasons
- Users who have reapplied multiple times unsuccessfully
- GrantCare users needing clear next steps

---

## 💰 Monetization Strategy
- Core product: R49 – R129
- Bundle with appeal + affidavit packs: R199+
- Upsell:
  - Done-for-you correction assistance (R200 – R400)
  - Priority support / review

---

## 📦 What’s Included (Product Contents)

### 1. Decline Reason Breakdown Guide

Explain the most common reasons clearly:

- Alternative income detected
- Identity verification failed
- Incorrect personal details
- Bank verification issues
- Duplicate or conflicting applications

---

### 2. Step-by-Step Fix Process

For each issue, provide:

- What the problem means
- Why it happens
- EXACT steps to fix it

---

### 3. Action Checklists

Simple checklists like:

- “Before you reapply, check this”
- “Fix your application in 5 steps”

---

### 4. Template Bundle (Core Value)

Include:

- Appeal letter templates
- Affidavit templates
- Motivation letter templates

👉 This product can bundle tools 5, 6, and 7

---

### 5. Reapplication Strategy Guide

Teach:

- When to reapply
- How long to wait
- What to change before resubmitting

---

### 6. Common Mistakes Section

Highlight:

- Submitting incorrect info
- Not updating details
- Using wrong bank accounts
- Ignoring rejection reasons
