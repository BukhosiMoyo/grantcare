# 📄 Affidavit & Proof of Income Template Pack (South Africa Optimised)

## 🧠 Overview
A professional collection of affidavit and proof of income templates tailored for South African use cases.

This product helps users quickly create legally acceptable documents for applications, including SASSA, jobs, housing, and financial support.

It removes confusion by providing **clear, correctly structured, ready-to-use documents**.

---

## 🎯 Target Audience
- Individuals applying for SASSA or financial assistance
- Job seekers needing proof of income
- People without formal payslips
- Users needing affidavits for official processes

---

## 📦 What’s Included (Product Contents)

### 1. Affidavit Templates (Editable)
- 5–10 templates
- Formats:
  - Microsoft Word (.docx)
  - PDF preview

---

### 2. Affidavit Scenarios Covered

Each template must reflect real needs:

- General affidavit (blank structure)
- Proof of unemployment
- Declaration of no income
- Supporting dependents
- Living arrangements confirmation
- Financial hardship declaration

---

### 3. Proof of Income Templates

For users without payslips:

- Informal income declaration
- Self-employed income statement
- Cash income explanation
- Support from family declaration

---

### 4. Fill-in-the-Blank Format

Make it simple:

- [Full Name]
- [ID Number]
- [Address]
- [Income Details]

👉 Must be editable in minutes

---

### 5. Instructions for Use (VERY IMPORTANT)

Include:
- How to complete the document
- Where to sign
- Where to get it certified (police station, etc.)
- What documents to attach

---

### 6. Example Completed Documents

Show:
- A fully filled affidavit example
- A sample proof of income document
