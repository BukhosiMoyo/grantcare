# 📄 Small Business Starter Pack (South Africa Optimised)

## 🧠 Overview
A complete starter kit designed to help individuals start a small business in South Africa with minimal confusion.

This product provides **ready-to-use templates, guides, and tools** to help users move from idea → structured business.

It is positioned as a **practical, beginner-friendly business launch kit**.

---

## 🎯 Target Audience
- Individuals wanting to start a small business
- Side hustle seekers
- Grant recipients looking to create income
- First-time entrepreneurs with no experience

---

## 💰 Monetization Strategy
- Starter pack: R99 – R299
- Premium bundle: R399 – R799
- Upsell:
  - Business consultation (R300+)
  - Done-for-you business setup assistance

---

## 📦 What’s Included (Product Contents)

### 1. Business Plan Template (Editable)

- Simple, beginner-friendly structure
- Sections include:
  - Business idea
  - Target market
  - Pricing
  - Costs
  - Marketing plan

Formats:
- Word (.docx)
- PDF preview

---

### 2. Startup Checklist

Step-by-step checklist:

- Register business (optional)
- Choose business name
- Set pricing
- Find customers
- Basic compliance steps

---

### 3. Pricing Calculator Template

Help users:
- Set correct prices
- Calculate profit
- Understand costs

---

### 4. Invoice Template

- Clean, professional invoice
- Editable format
- Ready to send to customers

---

### 5. Simple Marketing Guide

Explain:
- How to get first customers

- Basic social media strategy

### 6. Business Ideas List (SA Relevant)
