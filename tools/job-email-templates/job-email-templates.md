# 📧 Job Application Email Template Pack (South Africa Optimised)

## 🧠 Overview
A collection of ready-to-use, professional email templates designed for job seekers to apply for jobs quickly and effectively.

This product focuses on **real-world job application behavior**, where most users apply via email or WhatsApp and don’t know what to say.

The goal is to help users send **clean, professional, high-converting job application messages in seconds**.

---

## 🎯 Target Audience
- Job seekers applying via email daily
- Students applying for internships
- Individuals applying for multiple jobs quickly
- Users from GrantCare seeking employment

---

## 💰 Monetization Strategy
- Single pack: R29 – R79
- Bundle with CV + Cover Letter: R149 – R299
- Upsell:
  - CV review (R150+)
  - Done-for-you application setup (R200+)

---

## 📦 What’s Included (Product Contents)

### 1. Email Templates (Copy & Paste Ready)
- 10–15 high-converting templates
- Formats:
  - Word (.docx)
  - PDF version
  - Plain text (for direct copy)

---

### 2. Job Scenario Templates

Each email should match a real situation:

- Applying with CV attached
- Applying with no experience
- Follow-up after no response
- Asking if there are job openings
- Referral-based application
- Internship application
- Urgent job request

---

### 3. Subject Line Templates

Include strong subject lines:

- “Application for [Job Title] – [Your Name]”
- “Experienced [Role] Seeking Opportunity”
- “Job Application – Immediate Availability”

---

### 4. WhatsApp Application Templates (VERY IMPORTANT)

Include short, professional WhatsApp messages:

- Greeting + intention
- Short introduction
- Mention CV attached

👉 This is HIGHLY relevant in South Africa

---

### 5. Fill-in-the-Blank Structure

Make it extremely easy:

- [Your Name]
- [Job Title]
- [Company Name]
- [Your Experience]

👉 Users should edit in under 1 minute

---

### 6. Quick Guide (PDF)

Include:
- How to apply via email properly
- When to follow up
- Mistakes to avoid (e.g. long messages, poor grammar)
- Best time to send applications
